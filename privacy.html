Privacy

Last updated: June 2026

Christian History Daily is built to require as little of you as possible. There are no accounts, no email signups, no social features. This page summarizes what the app does and does not collect.

What we don't collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any account information — the app does not require an account.
  • We do not collect contacts, photos, microphone, camera, or location data.
  • We do not sell or share personal data with third parties for marketing.

What the app stores locally

Reading state — which cards you've seen, your interest preferences, and your premium-purchase status — is stored locally on your device. This data does not leave your phone.

Advertising

The free version of the app shows native ads on two of every five cards. Ads are served by Google AdMob. Ad personalization is controlled by Apple's App Tracking Transparency permission — if you deny tracking when prompted, ads will be non-personalized. You can change this at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

The one-time premium purchase ($9.99) removes ads permanently and is processed through Apple's standard In-App Purchase system via RevenueCat.

Crash and performance data

The app uses Apple's standard crash-reporting and performance frameworks. This data is anonymous and used only to identify bugs.

Children

The app is rated for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Contact

For questions about this policy, contact hello@christianhistorydaily.com.
